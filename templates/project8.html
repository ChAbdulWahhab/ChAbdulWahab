Wholesalers Management Dashboard for TWB

Project Overview

Built an advanced wholesalers dashboard for TWB to optimize supplier management and pricing strategy. The system allowed administrators to add and manage multiple wholesalers, compare their product prices in real time, and identify the most cost-effective vendors. It included intelligent tools such as an investment calculator and a product cost calculator to assist in informed decision-making and budget planning.
