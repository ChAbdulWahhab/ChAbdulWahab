Order Management System for TWB (Mobile Accessories Brand)

Project Overview

Designed and developed a robust order management system for TWB, a mobile accessories brand, to streamline order processing and tracking. The system featured two user roles: Super Admin and Delivery Personnel. The Super Admin could add new orders, assign them to delivery agents, and monitor their progress. Each order was auto-generated with a unique tracking ID, enabling customers to track their orders in real-time.
